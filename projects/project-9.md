## Fit By Jonetics: Your Complete Fitness Companion

Fit By Jonetics is a comprehensive fitness tracking application built for iOS and Android. The app helps you train smarter, track everything, and see real progress with workout logging, nutrition monitoring, and AI-powered features—all in a clean, fast experience built for daily consistency.

### Core Features

- **Workout Tracking**: Log exercises and track progress with 831+ pre-defined exercises across 5 categories—Glutes, Strength, HIIT, Functional Training, and Cardio.
- **Nutrition Tracking**: Monitor daily nutrition with barcode scanning, AI-powered food recognition, and comprehensive macro tracking.
- **Progress Analytics**: Advanced analytics with real-time data synchronization, progress monitoring, and personalized insights.
- **Personalized Workouts**: AI-powered workout generation tailored to your fitness level, goals, and preferences.

### Fitness Programs

- **Glutes** — Target your glutes with specialized exercises for strength and definition  
- **Strength** — Build muscle with comprehensive strength training exercises  
- **HIIT** — High-intensity interval training for calorie burn and cardiovascular fitness  
- **Functional Training** — Improve daily movement patterns and real-world performance  
- **Cardio** — Boost heart health and endurance with a diverse cardio selection  

### Technical Stack

- **Mobile**: React Native (iOS and Android)
- **Backend & Sync**: Firebase-powered real-time data synchronization across devices
- **AI**: Smart food recognition and personalized workout recommendations
- **Distribution**: App Store and Google Play
